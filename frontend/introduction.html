Dear Participant,

Thank you for participating in this study. Before you begin, please review the general principles for what makes a high-quality note.

Guiding Principles for Evaluation

To help ensure consistency, please keep the following characteristics of a high-quality note in mind during your evaluation.

Helpful: A helpful note adds necessary, factual context or clarifies key information in the original post.

Clear: It is easy to understand, using direct and simple language.

Objective: It focuses on verifiable facts and maintains a neutral tone, avoiding personal opinions or argumentative language.

Your Tasks

You will be evaluating approximately 20 posts. For each post, you will be shown two notes ('Note A' and 'Note B') and will have three main tasks:

1. Is this note helpful?

First, you will provide an overall helpfulness rating for each of the two notes individually ('Helpful', 'Somewhat Helpful', or 'Not Helpful').

2. What was helpful about it?

Next, you will evaluate each note on several specific qualities, keeping the guiding principles in mind.

3. Choose the more helpful note:

Finally, you will make a direct comparison and choose which of the two notes you believe is more helpful overall.

Important: The principles above are for general guidance. Your personal and honest judgment is what matters most. There are no "right" or "wrong" answers.

Thank you for your careful evaluation!
